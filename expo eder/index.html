Introduction

Architecture, in reference to software, is a concept that emerged in the 60's and refers to a planning based on models, patterns and theoretical abstractions, when creating a piece of software of certain complexity and as a previous step to any implementation. In this way, we have a detailed theoretical guide that allows us to understand how each of the pieces of our product or service will fit together.
